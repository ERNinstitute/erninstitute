Consulting & Collaboration

Through its consulting and collaboration programs, the ERN Institute provides expert guidance on experience-related neuroplasticity, bilingualism, cognition, aging, and research methodology for academic, clinical, educational, and industry partners.

Areas of Expertise

  • Experience-related neuroplasticity and cognitive reserve
  • Bilingualism, language experience, and cognitive control
  • Cognitive aging and dementia trajectories
  • EEG/ERP and cognitive neuroscience methods
  • Meta-analysis and quantitative synthesis
  • Measurement development and reliability

Who We Work With

  • Universities and research institutes
  • Educational organizations and school systems
  • Health and aging-focused organizations
  • EdTech, cognitive training, and related technology companies
  • Policy and non-profit organizations

Consulting Formats

  • Short-term advisory and project consultation
  • Evidence reviews and white papers
  • Study design and methodological guidance
  • Meta-analysis planning and interpretation
  • Workshops and invited talks
